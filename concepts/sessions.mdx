## What is a Session?

A Session is one real interaction between a user and an agent — a sequence of Turns and Tool Calls captured from production or staging. Sessions are ingested from your connected agent runtime (AgentCore, Salesforce, and similar platforms) and become the raw material QA Practitioners review, annotate, and curate into Datasets.

## How Sessions fit into the evaluation loop

Sessions sit at the start of the loop. You ingest them from the runtime, review and annotate to encode reviewer judgment, then curate the best examples into a Dataset that an Evaluation can run against.
