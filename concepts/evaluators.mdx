## What is an Evaluator?

An Evaluator is a reusable judgment function — typically an LLM Judge (plain-English criteria sent to Claude or GPT) or a Structured Match (deterministic checks on agent output metadata) — that scores a Turn or Session against criteria. Evaluators are defined once in your Project and reused across many Evaluations, so you can compare apples-to-apples across Dataset rows, Agent Versions, and time.

## How Evaluators fit into the evaluation loop

Evaluators are the judgment step. After you've curated a Dataset, you define one or more Evaluators that encode what passing looks like, then point them at the Dataset to produce a scored Evaluation run.
