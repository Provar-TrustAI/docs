## What is a Dataset?

A Dataset is a curated, versioned collection of Sessions or Turns chosen as a representative test bed. Rows in a Dataset can come from ingested Sessions, manual entries authored in the app, or CSV uploads. Datasets are what every Evaluation actually runs against — they're the stable substrate that lets you compare two Agent Versions or two Evaluators apples-to-apples.

## How Datasets fit into the evaluation loop

Datasets are the curation step. After you ingest and annotate Sessions, you pull the ones worth testing against into a Dataset, then point Evaluators at the Dataset to score every row in a single Evaluation run.
